About Me

It all started with a Spring semester internship at a small software company where I was responsible for testing a desktop application that was aimed at building usable dashboards. I was continuously amazed not only with the product, but with the process of actually being involved with building software.

Since then, I have worked with several companies including, Business Objects (an SAP company), Intuit, Zovio (former Bridgepoint Education), and more recently, ServiceNow.

While I've had a number of Software QA roles in the past, I've also worn the Software Developer hat. I've worked on variety of web applications, including Marketing-based websites designed to attract organic leads, a lead management application, and a university online application portal.

Outside of work, I enjoy watching live sports, including NBA, MLB, and Formula 1. I also enjoy the outdoors, spending time with my dog, and working on my cars.
